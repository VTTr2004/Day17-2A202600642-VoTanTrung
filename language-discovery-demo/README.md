# Language Discovery Demo

Static 2-phone simulator for three discovery prototypes:

1. Wizard of Oz Chat
2. Human Partner Pool
3. Omegle for English

Open `index.html` directly in a browser. No backend or install step is required.

## Demo style

One desktop screen shows two phone interfaces side by side.

- Phone A is usually Minh's account.
- Phone B is either the AI Coach/operator account or Linh's learner account.

Use the left sidebar to switch between the three prototypes.

## Prototype 1: Wizard of Oz

Phone A: Minh, the student.

Phone B: AI Coach, operated manually by the team.

Demo:

1. Minh sends an English sentence from Phone A.
2. The operator replies from Phone B using a quick reply or manual text.
3. Minh can tap Like as quick feedback.

Discovery signal:

- Do students keep practicing when they always receive a response?
- Do they care whether the responder is real AI?

## Prototype 2: Human Partner Pool

Phone A: Minh.

Phone B: Linh.

Demo:

1. Minh joins the partner pool from Phone A.
2. Linh joins the partner pool from Phone B.
3. Create a demo match after both accounts have joined.
4. Mark the session completed after the simulated call.

Discovery signal:

- Do students want a real human practice partner?
- Do they accept scheduled 10-15 minute practice sessions?

## Prototype 3: Omegle for English

Phone A: Minh.

Phone B: Linh.

Demo:

1. Minh taps Find Partner.
2. Linh taps Find Partner.
3. The app matches both accounts automatically.
4. Minh and Linh chat in English from the two phone screens.
5. End the session.

Discovery signal:

- Is instant partner availability the real pain point?
- How much does waiting affect the experience?
